All Things Fun!

Quilting, Pottery & Dog Training

Quilting allows me to express myself through color and geometry. Getting lost in sewing is peaceful and rewarding. For over 10 years, I have donated quilts to our Veterans and the Homeless.

All the dogs you see here have been rescued from kill-shelters. And most of the dogs you see hear came to me neglected, abused and with a lot of bad habits. The people helping these animals give them training through a positive reward system, unconditional love and a forever home through a vigourous screening process. Some dogs become therapy animlas for adults and children with seizures, PTSD, autism and much more.
